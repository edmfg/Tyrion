Tyrion — Plain Text Version

What Is Tyrion?

Tyrion is a new process for MFG. It creates visibility into how Google's Product Areas (PAs) overlap in audiences, partnerships, and messaging across markets.

Michael Bailey is changing how Media Lab works. Regional ML leads (Carrie, Emily, Sylvie) are rolling out these changes. Gemini is being integrated across many PAs, which makes overlaps worse.

Tyrion helps MFG see, manage, and untangle overlapping PAs within regions, markets, and across the world. It does this by mapping "domains" for each PA in each market, so anyone at MFG can see what every other PA is doing.

Key idea: Tyrion = giving everyone visibility into PA domain overlaps.

What You Need to Do

Task 1 — Keep the Tyrion Deck Updated

  • Update your slides when decisions are made (at least once a month). Open Tyrion Deck
  • Find your region and markets. Keep everything current. Leave a comment when you update a slide.
  • Slides should reflect MFG's recommendations. When ML/PMM signs off, mark with an asterisk (*) to show it's final.
  • Lighter green = lighter investment. Darker green = heavier investment. Two PAs can share a domain — the shading shows who's spending more.

Task 2 — Use Tyrion in PA Meetings & Decks

  • Check Tyrion when building strategy and media plans. Look for overlaps that create problems or opportunities.
  • In client meetings, Tyrion can go in the appendix. Just make sure clients know about it.
  • Add a Tyrion section to all major PA decks.

Remember: The goal is VISIBILITY, not policing Google.
